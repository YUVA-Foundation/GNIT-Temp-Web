About____

We are committed to nurture the required expertise under one roof by creating competent and motivated technocrats and managers of tomorrow, who will use their technical knowledge and managerial qualities to confidently and successfully lead the organization. For the overall development of the technocrats GNIT provided the best nurtured environment to the student by providing best facilities of hostel, transportation, cafeteria, soft skill training, book bank scheme and various curricular & extra curricular activities for the staff and students. Integrated teaching methodology is used for the enhancement of the students. Guest lectures from industry professionals and renowned academicians and industrial training tour are arranged to learn professional ethics.

Principal's message

Dear Readers,
Established in 2009, Guru Nanak Institute of technology has maintained its distinction in imparting quality education. Guru Nanak Institute of Technology Nagpur has been Equipping Students not Only with core as well as applied engineering curriculum to students. We apply various different techniques and strategies to impart latest education to the students. In recognition of the pioneering work done in the field of education. The world faces a tough situation with Covid-19 and the changes it brings especially in the education system. In such changing and challenging times, the proactiveness of staff determines the success in delivering quality education. It is here that the Motto of Guru Nanak Institute of Technology, Nagpur. is to impart quality, professional and technical education through experienced faculty and modern technology to the students for their Individual & Social Development.
This Pandemic in a way has opened up an opportunity to bring about changes in the teaching learning pedagogy and introduction of blended learning for the long term. The dedication and willingness of the staff to adapt to the changes will enhance best practices and also enrich the teaching, learning and research carried out in the college.

Vision

The constant Endeavour of Guru Nanak Institute of Technology is, to prepare professionally responsible and technically sound corporate citizens out of budding engineers.

____________
Mission

To educate students from all over India & other countries including especially those from the local & rural areas, so that they become enlightened individuals improving the living standards of their families, industries & the society.

To serve as a centre for fostering the co-operation and the exchange of ideas between the academicians and the research community on one hand and welcome the industrial community and the Government organizations to promote the entrepreneurship development among the students.

We will provide the world class quality education & pay serious attention towards the development of an individual for character building & the nation building.

To implement a program of education in Engineering Technology and management studies, relevant to the current needs of the industry, alive to the long term requirements and responsive to the anticipated changes and developments.

About The Institutions

Good education encompasses instilling moral, aesthetic, athletic and intellectual values in the citizens of tomorrow. GNIT Nagpur established in 2009 by GNES society is one of the top engineering colleges under RTMNU is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and Director of Technical Education, Maharashtra and NAAC Accredited. The college has lush green surrounding which provides conducive environment for students to grow not only as good Engineers but as world class citizens too. The campus is situated in the vicinity of MIDC Kalmeshwar which is the heart of the industrial zone. Spread over a sprawling 25 acres, the natural form of the landscape has been preserved.
