Computer Science Department____

CSE department is devoted for ensuring great careers to the students. This means moulding deeper industrial linkages than ever before, building research cultures from day one and promising flawless education by adapting the best technology available anywhere. The excellent teaching faculty and infrastructure of the best kind of CSE department make sure the quality education such as interaction among students, staff and parents, along with Training and Placement Cell guarantees a bright future to its students. CSE Department strongly encourage innovation in research, in teaching and in service to the profession, the local community and industry. Departmental faculty and students are constantly striving to excel and to advance the state of the art in Computer Science and Engineering.

AIM

  • Nurturing globally competent Computer Science & Engineering graduates by inculcating values of research qualities and society.
  • To impart high quality professional training with an emphasis on basic principles of computer science and engineering.
  • To strengthen links with industry through partnerships and collaborative development works.
  • To attain self-sustainability and overall development through research, consultancy and development activities.
  • To make the students as for as possible industry ready to enhance their employability in the industries.
  • To improve department industry collaboration through internship program and interaction with professional society through seminar/workshops.

Career prospect


Why Computer Science ?
Today, computers are used in almost every aspect of our lives: in car engines, microwave ovens, video games, watches, telephones, desktops at home and work, mainframe computers in government and industry, and super computers expanding the frontiers of science and technology. The computer industry is one of the fastest growing segments of our economy and that growth promises to continue well into the next century. Career Prospects

Software Developer
A software developer is one of the most important. It involves the fundamental task of creating software programs that allow the users of the software to do the desired job they want to do on the various peripherals they use. In simple terms, they create applications or program. Software engineers have the intellectual imagination they need to address app-specific challenges creatively. It is a requirement for students to be proficient in programming languages so that they can compose the ideal software code. Communication skills are essential to understand the user’s perspective on how the software works.

Computer Hardware Engineer
It is their job to ensure that the hardware is working correctly. A computer hardware engineer is determined to perform difficult challenges, including monotonous and comprehensive system testing. Most multinational companies are looking for competent personnel to make all systems work correctly and work on an up-to-date basis.

Computer Science Blogger
Computer science bloggers can earn with the help of publishing advertisements through Google AdSense and millions of other tools. Many people love to read blogs and use knowledge to seek job opportunities. It is the work of a blogger to help people build their careers while earning a considerable amount at the same time. PCB Designer This career prospect helps the graduates in mastering the software options with the assistance of which they design the PCB layouts with excellence. Many of the leading big gun companies are trying to find PCB designers to run.
