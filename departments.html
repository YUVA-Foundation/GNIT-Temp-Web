Departments____

Applied Science and Humanities (First Year)

First Year Engineering plays important role in building the foundation of an engineering graduate and learn basics of all branches along with Mathematics, Chemistry & Physics. We focus on the academic performance as well as industry interaction to develop overall personality of the students. The competent and enthusiastic faculty demonstrates unique team work for enhancing the academic and industrial knowledge among the students.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering was established in the year 2009, affiliated to RTMNU Nagpur. We have B.E. in Civil Enginerring & M.Tech. in Structural Engineering (Since 2014). The major strength of department is to have experienced & motivated faculty with good qualification (IIT’s, NIT’s & Reputed University) and working together as a team. Department has developed extraordinary Infrastructure, Class Room, Laboratories, CAD labs, Model Room etc. Department has strong links with construction Industries in the form of regular Industrial Visits, Guest Lecturers, and Summer Training for all students and value added workshop on CADs. Students of Civil Engineering Department also secured first rank in University Exam.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the principles of physics and materials science for analysis, design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is the branch of engineering that involves the production and usage of heat and mechanical power for the design, production, and operation of machines and tools. It is one of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines.

Computer Science and Engineering

CSE department is devoted for ensuring great careers to the students. This means moulding deeper industrial linkages than ever before, building research cultures from day one and promising flawless education by adapting the best technology available anywhere. The excellent teaching faculty and infrastructure of the best kind of CSE department make sure the quality education such as interaction among students, staff and parents, along with Training and Placement Cell guarantees a bright future to its students. CSE Department strongly encourage innovation in research, in teaching and in service to the profession, the local community and industry. Departmental faculty and students are constantly striving to excel and to advance the state of the art in Computer Science and Engineering.
